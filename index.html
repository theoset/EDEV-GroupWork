Our Story

Built in Botswana,
for Botswana.

Handled was born out of a simple frustration — finding a reliable, affordable local worker took too long and too many phone calls. We built a platform that makes it effortless: post a task, get matched with a verified worker nearby, and get it done. No middlemen, no waiting.

We started in Gaborone and we're growing fast. Our mission is to connect everyday households with skilled workers across Botswana — creating income for workers and peace of mind for customers.
